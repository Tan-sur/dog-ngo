Our Commitment

At Kindred Creatures, we believe every life deserves dignity and care. Our team of 150+ dedicated professionals and 500+ volunteers work tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals across India. Together, we're rewriting the story of street animals - one rescue at a time.
